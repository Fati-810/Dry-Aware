FAQ

1.What is Dry-Aware?

Dryaware is a system that uses a water meter and other hardware to measure and track the water of a household. Therefore, increasing water awareness and directing people to save water. It improves not only our environment, mitigates global warming but also saves the limited precious water resources, for our future generations to come.

2.How Does Dry-Aware System Works?

The system Dryaware works in the following way: The water meter measures the water consumption of the user. Which is sent to the cloud server using the raspberry pie. Then this data is retrieved by the application and shown to the user. After this, the mobile application uses this data to notify the user if they are using too much water. Also, if they are doing a good job and saving water.

3.What Other Functionality Then Water Monitoring Does Dry-Aware Application Provide?

It provides many functionalities such as short displays, report for the water consumption, tips to save water and responsibility increasing features like warnings & motivational messages. As well as giving weekly or daily (optional) current statistical updates.

4.When Does The Water Usage On My Application Reset?

Every night at 11:59 pm or after every 24 hours.

5.How Do I Get a Report?

To get a report: go to the Report Tab in the application. Then select a time span. Then click Generate Report Button. You should receive the report.

6.How To Get a Report For a Specific Time Period?

To get a report for a specific time period: go to the Report Tab in the application. Then select a time span by Inputting a “from” date to the “to”date. Then click Generate Report Button. You should receive the report.

7.What Can I Do To Support Water Conservation?

The Tips Tab in the application or How To Save Water page on website tells; what a person can do to support water conservation. You can help out by doing these tips yourself and having everyone close to you do the same. Also, you can help out greatly by increasing water awareness and sharing Dry-Aware.

8.How Does The Application Work?

The application has a side menu that can be used to navigate through the different parts of the application. The Home Tab shows the water consumption. The Detail Consumption Tab displays consumption in more detail. The Tips Tab shows the different categories, and upon selecting a category all the tips to save water in that category will be displayed. The Report Tab opens the page to generate reports.

9. What Is The Difference Between The Application and The Website?

The application shows the water consumption in a simple way. Its basic purpose is to notify the user according to there water consumption. When the user crosses a limit then the application will send a warning message. Also, when the user saves water, they will be appreciated. The website, however, does not do this. It includes How To Save Water, Contact Us page, About Us page, etc. And the purpose of the website is to thoroughly explain Dryaware to anyone who visits.

10.Where Do I Find My Profile and Settings?

In the Website, profile can be found in the Login Page and then View Profile. For Settings go to Your Account in Login Page.

11.How Do I Update My Details?

To update your details. Go to the Login Page then Your Account to update your details.

12.Where Do I Put Suggestions?

For suggestions please use the Contact Us page on the website. It also has the email and mobile number for our team. To enable you to contact with us, the team and to give us your suggestions. Each and every one of your suggestions are important to us.

13. I Have a Fault To Report, Where Do I Contact?

To report a fault, please use the Contact Us page on the website. It also has the email and mobile number for our team. To enable you to contact with us, the team and to give report any faults or feedback. Your feedback is highly valuable to us.
